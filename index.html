About Me

Hi, my name is Angel, a small town girl turned Front-End Web Developer based in Toronto. I take on new challenges with a positive approach and enjoy highly collaborative environments. Web development lets me combine the problem solving skills I gained from my background in Biochemistry with my creativity to build awesome web applications.

I’m excited to work on enhancing my Javascript knowledge and build more applications in React - looking forward to learning Node.js next! When I’m not coding, you can find me experimenting with leftover ingredients in the kitchen and singing and dancing my heart out .
